About me

I am Boyang Cheng, a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Notre Dame. My research lies at the intersection of digital and analog/mixed-signal circuit design, with a focus on developing energy-efficient and trustworthy hardware systems.

I am passionate about creating circuit and system solutions that bridge advanced digital architectures with robust analog and mixed-signal techniques. My work explores innovations that enable secure, privacy-aware, and low-power computation, particularly for edge devices and intelligent sensing applications.

With a background in both digital and analog/mixed-signal design, I enjoy tackling challenges that require cross-domain thinking and collaboration. My long-term goal is to advance hardware platforms that empower the next generation of intelligent, reliable, and resource-efficient technologies.
